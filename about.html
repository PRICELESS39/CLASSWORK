This is About Us Page

Welcome to Girls in Tech Nigeria, a vibrant and empowering community dedicated to bridging the gender gap in the technology industry. At Girls in Tech Nigeria, we believe in the transformative power of women in technology, and we are committed to fostering an inclusive and supportive environment for girls and women pursuing careers in STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics).

Our Mission

Empowering Women in Technology: Our primary mission is to empower and inspire girls and women to thrive in the technology sector. We are dedicated to breaking down barriers, fostering diversity, and providing resources and opportunities that encourage women to excel in tech-related fields.

What We Do

  • Educational Initiatives:
    • Girls in Tech Nigeria is at the forefront of educational initiatives aimed at equipping girls and women with the skills and knowledge needed to succeed in technology. From coding workshops to mentorship programs, we provide a range of opportunities to enhance technical expertise.
  • Community Building: We understand the importance of a strong support network. Through networking events, conferences, and meetups, Girls in Tech Nigeria creates a vibrant community where women can connect, share experiences, and build lasting relationships.
  • Advocacy and Awareness: We actively advocate for gender diversity in the tech industry and work towards raising awareness about the importance of inclusivity. Our campaigns seek to challenge stereotypes and create a culture that celebrates the contributions of women in technology.

Our Values

  • Inclusivity: We welcome individuals from all backgrounds, regardless of age, race, or experience level. Diversity is our strength, and we believe that everyone has a unique perspective to contribute.
  • Empowerment: We empower women to embrace their potential and pursue rewarding careers in technology. Through mentorship and support, we aim to build a generation of confident and capable tech leaders.
  • Innovation: We celebrate innovation and encourage our members to think creatively. By fostering an environment that values new ideas, we contribute to the advancement of technology and its positive impact on society.

Join Us on the Journey

Whether you're a seasoned professional, a student exploring tech possibilities, or someone curious about the tech world, Girls in Tech Nigeria welcomes you to join our community. Together, we can break down barriers, redefine norms, and shape a future where women thrive in technology.
